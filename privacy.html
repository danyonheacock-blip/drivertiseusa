Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Drivertise USA ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website and inquire about our services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our website at drivertiseusa.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly. When you fill out our contact or demo request form, we collect:

  • Your name and business name
  • Your phone number and email address
  • Your city and zone interest
  • Any message or additional information you include voluntarily

Information collected automatically. Our website may collect basic analytics data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Browser type and device type (desktop, mobile)
  • Referring website or search term
  • General geographic location (city/state level, not precise)

We do not currently use cookies beyond what is necessary for basic website function.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your demo request or inquiry
  • Schedule and conduct your free consultation call
  • Send you information about services you asked about
  • Follow up on our business relationship if you become a client

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Period.

Communications

If you contact us, you may receive a response via phone, text, or email depending on how you reached out and what you requested. We do not add you to automated email lists or send unsolicited marketing messages.

If you become a client, we will communicate with you about your active campaign — including weekly impression reports, billing updates, and any relevant changes to your zone or service. You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time by contacting us directly.

Data Storage and Security

Contact form submissions are delivered directly to our email inbox and are not stored in a third-party database. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any data transmitted to us.

We retain your contact information only as long as is reasonably necessary for business purposes — generally for the duration of our business relationship plus a reasonable period thereafter.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Fonts, loaded from Google's servers. Google's privacy policy applies to that service. We may use basic website analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate site traffic. These services may collect anonymous usage data. We do not configure them to collect personally identifiable information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information from our records
  • Opt out of any communications from us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@drivertiseusa.com or call (385) 204-6561. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
