How It Works

Reserve to mailbox — every step explained

1

Reserve your zone — 50% deposit

Pay half upfront to lock in your city zone and category exclusivity. No one else in your business category gets that zone while you're active.

2

Submit your design

Send your logo, copy, and offer — we'll format it to your ad size. Don't have a design? We can build it for you at an additional cost.

3

You approve a physical proof

Before anything goes to print, we produce one printed proof sheet and send you a photo. You sign off on it. No surprises at 10,000 copies.

4

Final payment → print → USPS

Once you approve, pay the remaining 50%. We print all 10,000 mailers, send you proof of purchase, then hand them off to USPS for delivery.

🎨

Need us to design your ad?

We can handle the design for you — logo placement, headline, offer layout, and formatting to the correct ad size. Ask Danyon for design pricing when you reach out. You'll still get a physical proof to approve before anything goes to print.
