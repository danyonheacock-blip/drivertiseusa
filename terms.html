Services Provided

Drivertise USA provides mobile advertising services including:

  • Car Wrap Zone Ads: Display of client-provided creative on the exterior of an Instacart delivery vehicle, driven through designated routes in the client's selected zone.
  • Electric Roof Billboard: Digital LED display mounted on the vehicle's roof, showing client creative on designated routes.
  • Direct Mail Co-op: Co-op advertising space in a targeted mailer delivered to homes within the client's zone area, on a bi-monthly cadence.

Specific terms for each service, including zone assignments, routes, impression estimates, and deliverables, are confirmed in writing (via email or service agreement) prior to campaign activation.

Billing and Payment

Month-to-month plans are billed at the start of each calendar month. Payment is due before campaign activation for the new billing period. Late payment may result in temporary suspension of campaign activity.

6-month plans are billed as a single upfront payment covering the full 6-month term. No refunds are issued for unused months if the client elects to end the campaign early.

Direct mail co-op billing follows a two-stage payment process:

  • 50% deposit is due at sign-up to reserve the client's zone and category exclusivity for the scheduled drop. This payment initiates the design and proof process.
  • We produce a single printed proof sheet and provide a photo for client review and approval before proceeding to full production. No full print run begins without client sign-off on the proof.
  • Remaining 50% is due after proof approval, before the full print run is authorized. Upon receipt of final payment, we print all 10,000 mailers, provide the client with proof of purchase from the printer, and submit the mailers to USPS for delivery.

If a client declines the proof and chooses not to proceed, the 50% deposit is non-refundable as it covers zone reservation, design, and proof production costs. Design services requested beyond basic ad formatting are quoted and billed separately, and are not included in the mailer space pricing.

Accepted payment methods will be confirmed during the onboarding process. All prices are in US dollars.

Cancellation Policy

Month-to-month clients may cancel by providing written notice (email or text is acceptable) at least 30 days before the next billing date. Campaign activity continues through the end of the paid billing period. No penalty fees apply.

6-month clients may not cancel mid-term without forfeiting the remaining pre-paid balance, as the discounted rate was contingent on the full commitment. Exceptions may be considered at our sole discretion in cases of documented business closure.

Upon cancellation, client creative is removed from the vehicle, tracking links are deactivated, and the zone is released back to the available pool.
