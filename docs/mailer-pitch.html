When to Add It

The sweet spot: 6–8 weeks into your car campaign.

By that point, residents in your target neighborhoods have likely seen the car multiple times. Your brand has begun to register — even if they haven't acted yet. When the mailer arrives at their home, it doesn't land cold. It lands on top of familiarity they already built from the car.

That second touch is what converts a vague impression into a real action — a call, a search, a visit. The car plants the seed. The mail delivers the harvest.
