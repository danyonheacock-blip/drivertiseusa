For month-to-month plans, you give 30 days written notice (text or email is fine) and your zone is released at the end of that billing period. No penalties, no collections, no awkward calls. For 6-month commitments, the full 6 months is billed upfront — that's how you get the discounted rate — but there are no auto-renewals that surprise you.
Each week you'll receive a simple summary covering: estimated impressions for the week based on routes driven, the locations hit (which grocery stores, streets, intersections), and any QR code scans or promo code uses if you opted into that. We use Google Timeline data to verify and log routes, and you can request access to verify it yourself.
Yes. For digital elements (QR codes, promo codes, landing pages), you can update your offer anytime with no charge. For physical vehicle wrap changes, we'll discuss options — minor updates can often be handled inexpensively, while full redesigns depend on the scope. 6-month plans include one creative refresh at month 3.
Typically 5–10 business days from the time you submit your creative and make your first payment. This allows time to finalize the wrap materials (if needed), set up your QR code and tracking links, and confirm your zone routes. If your creative is ready and it's a digital billboard or mailer, turnaround can be faster.
We don't guarantee a specific impression number, because impressions are affected by route traffic conditions that vary day-to-day. What we do commit to is driving your zone's designated routes every active day, providing transparent weekly reporting so you can see exactly what activity occurred, and prorating any days where driving didn't happen due to vehicle maintenance or illness.
