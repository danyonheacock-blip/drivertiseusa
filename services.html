Electric Roof Billboard

A dynamic LED display mounted on top of the vehicle. Your digital ad — static image, animated graphic, or video loop — rotates and plays as the car moves through your zone. Visible in daylight, striking at night, and updateable anytime without reprinting anything.

This is what makes Drivertise fundamentally different from a wrapped vehicle alone. The roof display catches attention the way a moving billboard should — especially at intersections, stop signs, and parking lots where eyes are already looking up.

Service 03

Direct Mail Co-op

A co-op mailer delivered to 10,000 targeted homes within your zone's geographic area. Your ad shares the mailer with 4–6 complementary local businesses — lowering cost for everyone while maintaining strong individual impact. Each category slot is exclusive: if you're the plumber, no other plumber goes in that mailer.

Direct mail has a 90%+ open rate compared to 20% for email. A physical piece in someone's hand, in their home, has staying power that digital can't replicate. Combined with a car wrap campaign in the same zone, you're covering both the street-level and the doorstep.
