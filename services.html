Electric Roof Billboard

A dynamic LED display mounted on top of the vehicle. Your digital ad — static image, animated graphic, or video loop — rotates and plays as the car moves through your zone. Visible in daylight, striking at night, and updateable anytime without reprinting anything.

This is what makes Drivertise fundamentally different from a wrapped vehicle alone. The roof display catches attention the way a moving billboard should — especially at intersections, stop signs, and parking lots where eyes are already looking up.

Direct Mail Co-op
